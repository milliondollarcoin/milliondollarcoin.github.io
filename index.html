Why we need it

The wealthy always had a way of protecting their wealth and had to rely on different physical assets like gold, art or properties. These assets had to be stored, maintained and protected and were prone to theft, deterioration and expropriation.

For these reasons the Million Dollar Coin was created, to provide a store of value without these risks of common assets. And like every asset, the worth can increase due to demand.

The Million Dollar Coin is the most scarce and unique store of value ever, with only one coin in existence with the initial worth of 1 Million Dollar.

Only 1 out of 7 Billion humans will ever be able to conveniently walk around with 1 Million Dollar all the time.

This is the safest store of 1 Million Dollar ever created.

How it works

The Purchase

The Million Dollar Coin will be based on the Ethereum Blockchain, it is premined and there will be only one Coin created.

Only individuals with an net worth of over 1 Million Dollar in Cryptocurrency will be eligible to participate in the Sale of the Million Dollar Coin.

The Sale of the Coin starts on the 01.12.2017 at 00:00 and 00 seconds UTC.

At this time we will mail all the participants the address where the 1 Million Dollar equivalent in Ethereum should be transferred to. Only one person will be able to purchase the One Million Dollar Coin, therefore as soon as the first transaction comes in other transactions are not possible anymore. If you transfer the Money after someone else, then your money will be transferred automatically back to your wallet, because the Million Dollar Coin wallet address declines further payments.

The Million Dollar Coin

The wallet address of the Owner of The Millon Dollar Coin is always engraved on the coin on www.themilliondollarcoin.io to state the current ownership of the coin.

The owner of The Million Dollar Coin will have additionally 140 letters for any message that he likes to be displayed on the homepage on www.themilliondollarcoin.io.

This message can be only altered whenever the coin changes its owner.

The Resale

The owner of The Million Dollar Coin will be able to sell the coin for whatever price he wants.

Once the owner changes a new wallet address will be engraved and a new message will appear on www.themilliondollarcoin.io
